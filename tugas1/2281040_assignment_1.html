What is UNAI?

Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by the Sevent-day Adventis Church of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was origanally called Opleiding Shcool der Advent Zending. in the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Sevent-day Adventis Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

  • 1) Faculty of Philosophy
  • 2) Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
  • 3) Faculty of Economics
  • 4) Faculty of Nursing
  • 5) Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
  • 6) Faculty of Information Technology
